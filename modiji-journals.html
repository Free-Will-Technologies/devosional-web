Modiji @ 75 – Work for Sanatan Dharma

Jai Hind Jai Bharat. I am Prof Dr N Ganga Vidya. I as a follower of Sanathan dharma think that Modiji is contributing towards the revival of the glorious Hindu Religion and culture. This year he is turning 75 years of age. I have written a small booklet requesting all people to respect him and appreciate all his efforts towards Sanathan dharma. I as a proud Indian think that he is one of the Yug Purush who is leaving very important footprints on the sands of the Sanathan time. His landmark efforts towards religion have been commemorated in this book. We have also translated (using AI and language editor friends) it into all the languages on the rupee note to reach across all states without any language barriers.
