Our Impact

Rewriting the Narrative of Rural Knowledge and Sustainable Development

At Sri Bala Gyanjivi Foundation, impact is more than numbers — it’s a transformational shift in how rural knowledge holders, indigenous communities, and entrepreneurs access opportunities, technology, and platforms for sustainable growth. Through strategic projects, digital knowledge platforms, and climate-focused interventions, we are making rural knowledge more inclusive, valuable, and globally accessible.

Our Reach

  • 775 Million Unlettered People Advocated Globally
  • 67 Women-led Ventures Created and Supported
  • 410 Entrepreneurship & Sustainability Programs Conducted
  • 12 Climate & Carbon Footprint Studies
  • 3 Project Expansions & Sub Units Created
  • 14 Doctoral Scholars Guided & Supported

What's Next?

As we move towards making rural knowledge a global currency of trust, Sri Bala Gyanjivi Foundation aims to:

  • Launch Gyanjivi, a digital currency for rural knowledge monetization.
  • Establish rural entrepreneurship hubs across India and beyond.
  • Build global alliances for climate resilience and rural knowledge digitization.
