Our Publications

Insights and Knowledge for a Sustainable Future

With a strong focus on climate action, ESG, education, and social entrepreneurship, Dr. N. Ganga Vidya has dedicated over 25 years to sharing knowledge that inspires change. Her publications span academic research, industry reports, textbooks, e-learning courses, and multimedia content, providing valuable insights for students, researchers, NGOs, entrepreneurs, and policymakers across the globe.

Featured Works

  • Textbooks: 23 textbooks published across disciplines, including management, entrepreneurship, and climate studies.
  • Journal Articles: 33 peer-reviewed journal articles addressing ESG practices, climate action, sustainable entrepreneurship, and inclusive education.
  • Conference Papers: 22 papers presented at national and international conferences on sustainability, climate change, and women entrepreneurship.
  • E-Learning Programs: 14 digital courses and online training materials focusing on ESG, climate action, digital literacy, and skill development.
  • E-Books: 22 e-books, including the notable “Modi@75”, translated into 21 languages for widespread accessibility.
  • Other Contributions: 25 brochures, 15 newsletters, and a range of online learning content that inspire across disciplines.

Making Knowledge Accessible

Through books, articles, online courses, and digital platforms, Dr. N. Ganga Vidya strives to make learning inclusive, actionable, and globally available. Her mission is to educate and empower future generations and underserved communities with tools, knowledge, and strategies that foster climate resilience, sustainability, and inclusive growth.

Discover thought-provoking works that drive change and inspire action across education, entrepreneurship, ESG, and climate action.
