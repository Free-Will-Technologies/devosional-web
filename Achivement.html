Our Achievements

“A Legacy of Excellence and Impact”

At Sri Bala Gyanjivi Foundation, every milestone is a testament to our dedication towards sustainable rural empowerment, climate action, and inclusive knowledge sharing. From national accolades to international recognitions, these achievements reflect our relentless pursuit of making rural knowledge matter globally.

Our Prestigious Awards & Recognitions

  • Mahatma Gandhi National Award (2024): Awarded by Socially Point Foundation (Niti Aayog & MSME) for excellence in rural knowledge dissemination.
  • Pride of India Award (2023): Recognized by Socially Point Foundation (Niti Aayog & MSME) for efforts in rural empowerment and sustainability.
  • I Prize World Documentary (2009): “Citizens of the World” by Les Production IDRC, featuring our founder’s efforts in rural knowledge and sustainable development.
  • Special Commendation from Prof. M.S. Swaminathan: Recognition for ICT4D & Climate Change Policy work.
  • Recognition from Nobel Laureate Prof. Joseph Stiglitz: Autographed copy of “Globalization and Its Discontents” for academic contribution and rural empowerment.
  • Feature in The Telecentre Times (Uganda): Highlighting founder’s efforts in rural digital literacy and entrepreneurship across global platforms.
