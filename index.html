SRI BALA GYANJIVI Foundation

Driving Sustainability, Education, and Social Impact for a Better World

Gyanjivi Organisation is a revolutionary digital platform founded by Dr. N. Ganga Vidya, a visionary educator, ESG expert, and climate action advocate. We combine digital libraries, multisensory learning, and innovative services to make knowledge universally accessible. Together, we create a world where education transcends boundaries, making a meaningful impact on society and the planet.

For over 25 years, Sri Bala Gyanjivi Foundation has worked to uplift underserved rural communities across India by making critical knowledge accessible and actionable. We believe every person, regardless of literacy or location, deserves the tools, information, and skills required to create sustainable futures for themselves and their families. Read More...

Vision

To bring peace, purpose, and profits to farmers, fisherfolk, livestock owners, entrepreneurs, and indigenous communities, making their undervalued contribution a cornerstone of global prosperity.

Mission

To create a global knowledge-sharing ecosystem that empowers the unlettered and underserved, making knowledge inclusive, accessible, and self-sustaining. Our goal is to enable rural communities to ride the wave of digital technologies and transform information accessibility within the next three years.

About the Founder

Dr. N. Ganga Vidya is a passionate educator, ESG consultant, and climate action expert with 25+ years of experience. Her vision has shaped sustainable policies, empowered women entrepreneurs, and inspired countless lives across disciplines. Read Her Story...

Together for a Sustainable Future

The future belongs to those who make knowledge inclusive. At Sri Bala Gyanjivi Foundation, we stand with rural communities, farmers, fisherfolk, entrepreneurs, and indigenous people, bringing forth a world where heritage, knowledge, and technology unite.
